About Green Golf Carbon

At Green Golf Carbon, we are redefining turf management. Our patented basalt blend leverages enhanced rock weathering (ERW) to naturally capture atmospheric CO₂, improve soil nutrient profiles, and reduce chemical inputs.

Backed by rigorous field trials and research showcased at AGU and in leading journals, our solution replaces inert quartz sand with a nutrient‐rich basalt blend – making golf courses and turf facilities greener and more sustainable.

Our Process

Our innovative basalt blend harnesses enhanced rock weathering to improve soil quality and capture atmospheric CO₂.

CO₂ + H₂O + Basalt → Nutrients + Bicarbonate

  • Improve soil fertility and reduce the need for chemical fertilizers
  • Lower soil acidity by acting as a natural lime replacement
  • Release essential minerals (K, P, Ca, Mg, etc.) into the soil
  • Sequester atmospheric CO₂ as stable bicarbonate
